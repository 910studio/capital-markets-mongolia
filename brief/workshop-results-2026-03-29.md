## Killed Directions

- **Mono / Ledger / Blueprint** — Hard no. "Too general."
- **Pastel soft tones** — No.

---

## Client Mandates

1. **BRANDING** is the #1 priority — identity, color, brand loyalty (newsletter, email, etc.)
2. **Motion + micro-interactions** — must-have, not nice-to-have
3. **User access hierarchy:** Public → Registered → Registered + Paid (3-tier)
4. **Mix of Western/Eastern UI design** — both influences
5. **Data-dense UI** — finance bros love it, question is serving general users too
6. **Simple gradients** — they love them
7. **Colorful** — not muted, not monochrome

## Content Architecture

| Type | Treatment |
|------|-----------|
| **News** | AI-aggregated, portal-style (Yahoo Finance), no dedicated imagery |
| **Articles** | Written content, focus area |
| **Insider News** | Focus area, distinct from regular news |

News ≠ Article — these are distinct content types.

---

## Design Direction Variants (post-workshop)

Based on workshop feedback, 3 refined variants built from the approved CMM Light + Atlas base:

1. **Alpha** — AlphaSense-inspired. Pure white, maximum readability, structural purple.
2. **Signal** — Brand-forward creative. Warm surfaces, gradient CTAs, orange pops, more color presence.
3. **Dense** — Pitchbook/Bloomberg data mode. Tighter metrics, compact spacing, info-density maximized.

All use Plus Jakarta Sans 800 (display) / DM Sans 400 (body) / JetBrains Mono (mono).
